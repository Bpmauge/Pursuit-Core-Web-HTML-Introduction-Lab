The Ultimate Curry Goat

Decription

This is a delicious, slow cooked dish that is full of flavor and tender to the bone! A Caribbean cuisine staple thats an absolute must-make!

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs goat meat, cut in chunks
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 6 Tbsp curry powder
  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, sliced and seeded (handle with care)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • water

Directions

  1. Mix goat meat, salt, pepper, garlic, scotch bonnet, 1/2 onion and 4 Tbsp of curry powder together and marinate in the fridge for at least 5 hours.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated goat meat (remove the scotch bonnet pepper) to the pot and brown to seal in juices.
  3. Once meat is browned, pour just enough water to cover the meat. Bring the pot up to a boil, then let it simmer about 1 & 1/2 hours, until tender.
  4. Add potatoes and the rest of the curry powder, and simmer for 30 minutes until potatoes tender.

Need a side dish? Try serving with rice and peas
